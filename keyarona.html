PORTFOLIO

  • KEYARONA SHOPPING CENTERE


    Located on the R556 off N4 in the greater municipal area of Madibeng. It is approximately 70kms from Sun City and situated between Brits and Rustenburg in the North West Province

    RPIH credits this shopping centre as its breakthrough in the property development industry. It boasts a great number of well-known national retailers such as Spar, (Moratiwa Development Property in partnership with Vukile Property Fund).

    This area is buzzing with platinum mining activities and supporting activities. National tenants such as Shoprite, Edgars, Mr. Price, KFC, Express Stores, Edgars Active, Dunns, Capitect and many reputable retailers and service providers are housed at Keyarona Shopping Centre servicing the Bapong, Marikana and surrounding areas.
