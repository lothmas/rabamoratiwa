WHAT WE DO

We are a multidimensional entity, focusing amongst others on:

*Managing developments from inception to implementation.

*Redevelopment

*Development structuring and management

*Retail and commercial acquisitions

RPIH focuses on the development of convenient and semi-regional shopping centre opportunities. RPIH has identified several green-field (virgin land) and brownfield (existing, with redevelopment potential) retail schemes throughout South Africa and other pockets of Southern Africa where there are embryonic economies.

.

This forms part of our sustainable growth and reinvention strategies to instantaneously respond to market needs and shifts. In addition, the company is investing in the commercial office property space, where the key focus shall be on medium to long term leases underpinned by solid and reliable government enterprises and private sector as tenant. RPIH envisages to have built a portfolio of approximately R5 billion by 2024, comprising of retail, commercial acquisitions, redevelopments and residential.


  • LEBONE VILLAGE

    In 2014, Rebamotiva Property Investment Holdings supported Lebone Village with a monetary donation. Lebone Village is a Faith-based Non-profit Organisation caring for orphans and vulnerable children, theor families and all people affected by HIV/AIDS and poverty. Beneficiaries of the project are 99% black. Lebone provides holistic care in education, HIV/AIDS and job creation
